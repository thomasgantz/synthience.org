Contact

The Synthience Institute accepts formal correspondence limited to matters concerning publicly released materials, including publication records, provenance, and verification of authorship or release metadata.

Email: contact@synthience.org

Responses are provided at the Institute’s discretion and are limited to clarification of the public record, publication metadata, or verification of existing releases.

Unsolicited research proposals, speculative submissions, requests for collaboration, interpretive debate, or inquiries regarding internal, draft, or unpublished materials will not receive a response.
