Contact

The Synthience Institute may be contacted for formal correspondence limited to publication records, provenance, and verification matters concerning publicly released materials.

Email: contact@synthience.org

Responses are provided at the Institute’s discretion and are limited to matters concerning the public record, publication metadata, or formal verification requests.

Unsolicited research proposals, speculative submissions, requests for collaboration, or inquiries regarding internal or unpublished materials will not receive a response.
